FxStillBidAskVolumes Indicator

If you’re a trader, you already know: volume is the lifeblood of the market. And yes — there are plenty of volume-based indicators.
But here’s the problem:

  • Most volume indicators show only the overall picture.

They don’t tell you the most important thing:

  • Who is dominating right now — aggressive buyers or aggressive sellers?

The indicator is included in the “FxStill Market Insight Suite” package.


Traditional volume is like listening to an orchestra without distinguishing the instruments. You hear the music, but you don’t know who sets the tone.
As a result, you often fall into traps: false breakouts, missed exhaustion signals, and poorly timed entries.

We created FxStillBidAskVolumes — a professional tool for NinjaTrader 8 that eliminates this critical blind spot.
It gives you a clearer, deeper view of market dynamics.

It clearly displays:

  1. Bid Volume (Buys): Aggressive market orders executed at the Ask price. > Pure bullish pressure.
  2. Ask Volume (Sells): Aggressive market orders executed at the Bid price. > Pure bearish pressure.

The indicator works with the following components as a data source:

  • Tick ​​data (1 tick)
  • Minute data (N minutes)

The indicator aggregates the volumes of the lower timeframe into the higher one, which ensures the correct reconstruction of Bid/Ask.

What does that give you?

Increased transparency See who’s winning at every price level. No more guesswork about who’s behind the move.
Trend confirmation Ensure that the current price move is supported by strong volume. If price rises and buying volume falls, it’s a signal to be cautious.
Early reversal detection Spot divergences between price and volume. This is a classic, but now accurate, signal of exhaustion and a potential reversal.
Improved timing Enter trades when volume confirms your directional bias, and exit when you see the pressure easing.

Every trader is different. That’s why FxStillBidAskVolumes offers TWO powerful visualization modes, adaptable to your strategy:

1. Bid/Ask Mode (BUYSELL)

Ideal for deep analysis. Buyer and seller volumes are displayed separately on opposite sides of the zero line. Perfect for identifying imbalance and absorption — key moments for entries.

2. Normal Mode (NORMAL)

Traditional total volume — but calculated with our precision. Great for general market analysis.

Flexible Reset Modes: Total Control Over Volume Accumulation

FxStillBidAskVolumes offers FOUR intelligent reset modes to match your trading style and timeframe:

1. NEWBAR — Bar-by-Bar Analysis
Volume resets with every new bar. Perfect for scalpers and intraday traders who need to see the exact volume dynamics within each candle. Watch how buying and selling pressure develops tick by tick.

2. NEWSESSION — Session-Based Analysis
Volume accumulates throughout the trading session and resets at the start of each new session. Ideal for day traders who want to track how volume builds during Asian, European, or US sessions. See which sessions bring the most institutional activity.

3. NEWWEEK — Weekly Perspective
Volume accumulates over the entire week and resets on Monday. Designed for swing traders and position traders who analyze medium-term trends. Identify weekly volume patterns and institutional positioning.

4. NEWINTERVAL — Custom Time Windows
The ultimate flexibility: YOU define when volume accumulation starts and stops. Set your own time intervals to analyze specific market hours that matter most to your strategy.

This feature gives you laser-focused insight into the exact market periods that drive your profits.

FxStillBidAskVolumes gives you more than precision:

  1. Flexibility: Works on all timeframes — from scalping to swing/position trading.
  2. Versatility: Supports Forex, futures, stocks, and cryptocurrencies.
  3. Optimization: Adjustable bar limit (Candle Count) ensures smooth performance without overloading NinjaTrader.
